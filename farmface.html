Farm Fresh

FARM FACE

Farm fresh is a project that focuses on empowering the farmer and directly connecting them with their consumers. Such a platform benefits the producer i.e the farmer and the consumer by providing a clear line of communication between the two from the comfort of their homes. The consumer gets fresh and high quality products at a wholesale price and the farmer reduces their selling time by eliminating intermediary channels and increasing their profit margins. Keeping a track of the crops that sell more the farmer can access and plan ahead to decide which crop they need to grow.

Design Process

I started the project by understanding the current market scenarios and researching/interviewing farmers and their pain-points. Based on the analysis of these interviews, the lead time to sellings the entire stock was a major issue faced by 95% of the farmers that I interviewed. On brainstorming and ideation I released that this delay is caused due to multiple intermediary channels through which the crops goes before reaching the final consumer hence inflating the price of the crop and the lead time for the farmer. Based on this I designed multiple wireframes and task flows, breaking down other tasks that could also be included into the application to provide a better user experience. After which I tested out low-fidelity prototypes, finalising on one based on user feedback.

Task - To search for a farmer :

The task that is shown here demonstrates how the consumer selects a farmer from whom they might want to purchase a crop from. There is an onboarding page that leads to the home page from where the consumer can branch out to multiple tasks based on their preference. The home page has a search bar that aids the consumer to search for a farmer based on different filters like distance, time of delivery etc, upon which the application shows a list of farmers based on the filters to choose from. Hence connecting the consumer directly with the the farmer.
